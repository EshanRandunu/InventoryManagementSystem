Option 3: Use the Services Panel (Cleanest)
Since it's a Tomcat service, killing it via the UI is often more reliable:

Press Win + R, type services.msc, and press Enter.

Find Apache Tomcat 9.0 in the list.

Right-click it and select Stop.

To prevent it from coming back after a restart, right-click it → Properties → Change Startup type to Manual.
